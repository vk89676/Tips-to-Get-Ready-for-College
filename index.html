Tips to Get Ready for College

By: Vaibhavi Kulkarni

Getting ready for college is an extremely important task. This is one of the first steps an individual takes into adulthood. The process of appyling is very time-consuming with the applications and essays, so here are a few tips to keep you on track!

The Main Sections

  • Start Early
  • Importance of Grades
  • Writing College Essays
  • Scholarships

When Should I Begin?

There is no set time for one to begin looking for colleges that match their intersets. It can be started from your elementary school years. Also, when it comes to writing your applications and essays, it is best to start ahead and give yourself ample amounts of time to work. Basically, it's always best to start soon.

Grades

Grades are indeed a big part in applying for colleges. Although, this is not the only criteria that gets judged, it is still one of them. So, always keep in mind to try your best in academics. It will make you a better and smarter person, and colleges would be impressed. Saying this, it is not suggested to stress about grades. If they are not perfect, that's okay! Keep attempting and try to excel in other areas, such as extra- curriculars.

Writing College Essays

Writing essays in English class can be pretty stressful at times, and it is no different with writing college essays. That is why it is reccommended to start ahead of time, so that you can keep editing and editing. This way, your brain won't be under too much pressure about the deadline, and you can work with a fresh mind everyday. Another helpful thing to do, is having multiple people check your essays. At times, one can't see their own errors, but other people can easily spot them!

Scholarships

Schloarships are another massive part of applying for colleges. When applying, obviously everyone wants to apply for the high rated schools. But, not eveyone can afford the school's tution fees, lodging fees, food, and material (textbooks, books, etc.). In these cases, scholarships can be really handy! Not to mention, it is very simple to sign up! All you have to do is...

  1. Search for Scholarships
  2. Find Some that Seem Interesting
  3. Check Their Guidelines (what is the task being asked)
  4. Submit your Response, and Wait

Some Suggestions...

This whole process may seem a little overwhelming, but always remember to start early so that possible stress can be avoided. Also, take the help of whomever you can. This includes family, friends, adults, counselors, and teachers. The more help you get, the more satisfied you may be with the results. So, don't hesitate to seek out for help. Last but not the least, don't forget to enjoy the whole experience, and spend time with your loved ones.
